Minions

The most stupid ones

Kevin

Kevin is a tall, two-eyed minion with sprout cut hair and is usually seen wearing his golf apparel.

He appears in Despicable Me 2, Despicable Me 3, Training Wheels, the prequel films Minions and Minions: The Rise of Gru, Minions Paradise, and Minions Oscars Segment 2016. A minion by the same name, having a different appearance, appears in the first film.

Stuart

Stuart is a one-eyed short Minion with combed hair.

He appears in Despicable Me, Despicable Me 2, Minions, Minions: The Rise of Gru, Orientation Day, Minions Oscars Segment 2016, Minions Paradise, Santa's Little Helpers. and he is in the opening and ending of Despicable Me 4.

KING BOB

Bob is a short heterochromatic bald minion. He often carries around a teddy bear that he owns called Tim, which is brown with yellow buttoned eyes.

He first appears in Despicable Me 2, the opening of Despicable Me 3 and the prequel films Minions and Minions: The Rise of Gru. and the opening and ending of Despicable Me 4.
